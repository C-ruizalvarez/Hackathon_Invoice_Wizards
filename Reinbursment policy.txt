The company reimbursement policy states:
- Meals are reimbursable only if they are work-related and under $20.
- If meal is with a client, then reimbursable if under $150 per person.
- Sales tax is reimbursable if the main expense is eligible.
- Alcohol is not reimbursable.
- Tips are reimbursable if under 20% of the total of the invoice.
